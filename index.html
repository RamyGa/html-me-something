Habitable planets in the known universe

For every star we see in the observable universe there is at least one planet revolving around its host star, that means there are more planets in the universe than there are grains of sand on earth. This means that some of those planets are revolving around their host star at the right distance for life to exist. Some of those habitable planets have been found, below you'll see some of those habitable exo-planets. Hover over a planet to learn more about it.

Hover over a card below to reveal data about the planet!
